Services

Our company is a leading specialist in deep foundation works and geotechnical engineering solutions, delivering excellence through the use of advanced equipment and modern technologies. We provide innovative, reliable, and cost-effective solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and safety..

Rotary Bored Piles

Executed using bentonite-water slurry and full-length reinforced concrete. Suitable for bridges and specialized structures. Diameters: 60 cm – 250 cm Depths: up to 55 meters

Continuous Flight Auger (C.F.A.) Piles

Efficient pile installation method with minimal vibration and noise. Diameters: 40 cm – 120 cm

Retaining Walls & Secant Piles

Constructed using bentonite and concrete to provide reliable excavation support.

Micropiles & Ground Anchors

Designed for structural strengthening, underpinning, and retaining wall stabilization.

Soil Improvement & Grouting Works

Advanced soil injection techniques to enhance soil properties and ensure ground stability.
